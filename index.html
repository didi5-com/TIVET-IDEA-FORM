Federal Ministry of Education

Code of Conduct on Gender-Based Violence (GBV), SEA & Sexual Harassment

I acknowledge that preventing Gender-Based Violence (GBV), Sexual Exploitation & Abuse (SEA), and Sexual Harassment is important. I understand that GBV/SEA/SH constitutes gross misconduct and may lead to sanctions, termination, or prosecution under Nigerian law. I agree that during my engagement under the IDEAS Project, I will: • Maintain respectful relationships • Not engage in sexual exploitation or abuse • Not engage in sexual activity with minors • Attend GBV/SEA/SH training when required • Report any incidents through official GRM channels I understand the sanctions applicable to: • Contractors • Trainers • Trainees • Support Staff • NPCU Staff This Code aligns with Nigerian law and World Bank Environmental & Social Standards.
